Educational Guides

Government Subsidy Information

  • PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana: A government scheme offering financial assistance for solar rooftop installations.
  • Eligibility Criteria: Residential homeowners and small commercial establishments.
  • Application Process: Fill the online application, submit documents, and get approval from government authorities.
  • Subsidy Amount: Up to ₹78,000 depending on system capacity and electricity usage.
